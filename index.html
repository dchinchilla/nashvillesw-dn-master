Techstars Nashville Startup Week is dedicated to bringing the entire entrepreneur ecosystem together for a week of education, collaboration and connection. Whether you want to be an entrepreneur one day or you’ve already done it successfully a number of times this event is catered to you.

The Venues

Meet us for talks at the Baker Donelson Center M, W-F 3:00-6:30.

Networking & Pitch Events at Industrious M&T 7-9p.

Pitch Events at the EC W 7:00-9:00 and the Wondry Th 7:00-9:00.

FAQ

What is the purpose of this event?
The purpose of Nashville Startup Week is to bring the entire startup ecosystem together for Education, Collaboration & Connection.
Who should attend this event?
Everyone! Whether you think you might want to be an entrepreneur one day or you've already done it successfully numerous times.
What is the format of the event?
The event is made up of 5 tracks, Healthcare, Music, Data/Development, Business 101 and Funding 101. Each day we will have a 30 minute talk from an experienced entrepreneur in each track. At the end of the day we will move from the speaking venue to one of Nashville's best co-working spaces for pitch nights & networking events.
What time is the event?
The event is March 13 - 17 starting at 3:30 each day running from 3pm until 9pm.
How Much does it Cost?
This event is totally free for everyone!
What will I get out of the event?
It's our goal that you will learn a ton, meet awesome people & foster relationships for business opportunities in Nashville..
